Reflection

Working on this project helped me understand how AI can improve the hiring process, especially when it comes to finding qualified candidates from outside a company. One challenge I had was figuring out how to explain the AI system in a simple way without making it too complicated. Another one was trying to make a site with GitHub. I never really did it before so learning how to do it took some research. I had to look up videos on youtube and even ask a friend of mine who is big into using GitHub. I also learned more about how hiring actually works in businesses and how AI can make it faster and more efficient. AI helped me organize my ideas and build the structure of my project but I made sure I understood everything I included. At the end of it all, this project helped me combine technology with real-world business problems.
